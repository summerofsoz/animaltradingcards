Gerberian Shepsky

The Shepsky is a designer breed combining the best of the German Shepherd and Siberian Husky breeds in terms of temperament and intelligence.

  • Scientific Name: Canis lupus familiaris
  • Average Weight: 45 to 88 pounds
  • Average Lifespan: 10 to 13 years
  • Habitat: This breed is a high energy working dog that requires a home with a yard or other wide open spaces such as a dog park.

This Shepsky, named "Nymeria", leans toward the more Siberian Husky part of her genetics. She has the blue eyes of a Husky. Although highly intelligent, she leans toward the independent nature of a Husky and can resist commands. She's motivated by food, rarely barks, and is friendly toward humans and dogs alike.
